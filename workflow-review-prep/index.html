Workflow review prep

Bring the workflow, not a polished deck.

A good review call traces what happens from the first source report to the morning decision. The messier the real workflow, the more useful the call.

Book a timeEmail the workflow

What to bring.

Do not overprepare. The goal is to see the current process clearly enough to scope one narrow pilot.

01

The final output

The spreadsheet, email, dashboard screenshot, PDF, or deck people actually use in the morning.

02

The source reports

PMS exports, pickup files, forecasts, rate shops, channel reports, and recurring inbox/folder sources.

03

The manual steps

Copy/paste, formulas, edits, formatting, checks, comments, screenshots, and exception notes.

04

The timing rules

When files arrive, when the brief is needed, and what happens when something is missing or late.

05

The owner

Who assembles it, who reviews it, who acts on it, and who gets blamed when it is wrong.

06

The first win

The smallest reliable output that would make the team say, “we should not go back.”
